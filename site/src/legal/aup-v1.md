# RoamCode Cloud Acceptable Use Policy

Effective: 17 July 2026 · Version 1.0

You may use RoamCode only for systems, accounts, repositories, and data that you are authorized to access.

You must not use the service to:

- compromise, probe, or disrupt systems without the owner’s informed permission;
- distribute malware, credential stealers, ransomware, destructive payloads, or deceptive updates;
- bypass access controls, monitoring, rate limits, sanctions, or legal restrictions;
- facilitate unlawful surveillance, harassment, exploitation, or trafficking;
- expose another person’s secrets, source code, personal data, or confidential information without authorization;
- operate a public relay, resale offering, or competing hosted control-plane using private RoamCode service components without a written commercial agreement;
- send spam or use invitations and transactional email for marketing; or
- interfere with service integrity, other tenants, or incident investigation.

Security research must be performed against systems you own or under a published authorization program. Use test data and minimize impact.

RoamCode may rate-limit, isolate, or suspend activity that creates an immediate security or legal risk. Reports are reviewed in context, and users may contact https://roamcode.ai/contact to appeal an enforcement decision.
